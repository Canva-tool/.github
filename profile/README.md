## Overview

**Canva** is a flexible design environment created to simplify visual content creation for digital and print use. It allows users to build graphics, presentations, layouts, and marketing materials through an intuitive, layout-based workspace.

The platform focuses on accessibility and speed, making it easy to transform ideas into polished visuals without complex setup. **Canva** supports a wide range of creative tasks, from quick social visuals to structured brand assets.

With its organized editor and adaptable tools, **Canva** fits both individual creators and collaborative teams working on consistent visual output.
